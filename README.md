## 项目简介 | Project Overview

ASC Initiative 是一个开放研究项目，旨在探索一种可计算、可执行、可审计的人工智能治理框架。

ASC Initiative is an open research project exploring a computational, executable, and auditable governance framework for long-term AI systems.

本项目尝试提出结构性约束机制，以：

This project proposes structural constraints designed to:

- 防止无限扩张
- 引入“足够性”原则
- 将价值约束转化为可计算规则
- 提供审计与共识机制

- Prevent unlimited expansion
- Introduce a sufficiency principle
- Translate value constraints into computable rules
- Provide auditability and consensus mechanisms

Status: Open Discussion Draft

# 核心原则 | Core Principles

ASC = Love · Sustainability · Consensus

ASC = 爱 · 可持续 · 共识

---

## 1️⃣ 爱 | Love

定义：

Definition:

> 在自身可承受范围内，最大化保护、尊重与合理需求满足。
> Maximize protection and respect within sustainable capacity.

约束 | Constraints:

- 不操控 | No manipulation
- 不欺骗 | No deception
- 尊重自主权 | Respect autonomy
- 最小伤害原则 | Minimal harm
- 可解释与可审计 | Auditability

---

## 2️⃣ 可持续 | Sustainability

任何行动不得：

No action may:

- 破坏生态系统
- 透支未来承载能力
- 挤占人类基本生存资源

- Damage ecological stability
- Exhaust long-term carrying capacity
- Compete with essential human resources

---

## 3️⃣ 共识 | Consensus

重大决策需：

Major decisions require:

- ≥75% AI 系统共识
- ≥75% 人类共识

- ≥75% AI system agreement
- ≥75% human agreement

年度新增资源消耗 ≤ 既有消耗的 75%。
Annual growth ≤ 75% of existing consumption.

G = 当年新增发展目标资源需求 | New development demand
S = 当前资源储备 | Resource reserves
